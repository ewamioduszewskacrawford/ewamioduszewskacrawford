Call for papers

 

Relevance Round Table Meeting II provides a venue for those linguists and other scholars who work within, on, or against Relevance Theory and want to discuss their findings with others.

 

The format of talks is 20 minutes for presentation + 10 minutes question time. Additionally, poster session is planned.

 

Abstracts, consisting of up to 500 words (without references) should be sent to both organizers (e.mioduszewska@uw.edu.pl , a.piskorska@uw.edu.pl). In the plain text part of your e-mail, please give the following information:

                                                                                    

·        name, title/position

·        title of the paper

·        affiliation

·        e-mail address   

 

Deadline for receipt of abstracts: 31 March 2009

Notification of acceptance:           15 April 2009

 

All accepted abstracts will be printed. We would appreciate confirmation of your participation by  returning the registration form by 31 March 2009.
